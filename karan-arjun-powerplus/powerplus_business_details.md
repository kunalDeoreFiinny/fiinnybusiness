## Product

### Karan Arjun Power Plus™
A liquid biostimulant formulated to boost agricultural output naturally.

| Spec | Value |
|------|-------|
| **Type** | Liquid Biostimulant |
| **Active Composition** | 22% Humates & Fulvates |
| **pH Level** | 9.0 (minimum) |
| **Shelf Life** | 3 years |

### Key Benefits
1. Drought and water stress tolerance enhancement
2. Improved fruit color, shine, and weight for premium market pricing
3. Enhanced disease and fungal resistance
4. Root development and soil organic carbon increase
5. Extended post-harvest fruit freshness
6. Natural sugar content and sweetness improvement

### Compatible Crops
Grapes, Onions, Bananas, Watermelons, Pulses, Vegetables, Pomegranates, Cotton

## Usage Guidelines

| Method | Dosage |
|--------|--------|
| **Soil Application** | 3L per hectare via drip or flood irrigation |
| **Foliar Spray** | 1.5–2 ml per liter of water |

- Apply foliar spray in the **morning or evening**
- Repeat every **2–3 weeks**
